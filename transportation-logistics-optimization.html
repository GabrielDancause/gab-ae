Optimization Strategies

Cost Reduction Tactics

  • Consolidation: Combine shipments to reduce transportation costs
  • Backhauling: Optimize return trips to minimize empty miles
  • Modal Shift: Choose optimal transport modes for cost efficiency
  • Network Design: Strategic facility placement and routing
  • Carrier Negotiation: Leverage volume for better rates
  • Fuel Optimization: Route and speed optimization for fuel savings

Service Improvement

  • Real-time Tracking: Enhanced visibility for customers
  • Predictive ETAs: Accurate delivery time estimates
  • Exception Handling: Proactive issue resolution
  • Flexible Delivery: Multiple delivery options and time slots
  • Communication: Automated updates and notifications
  • Returns Management: Streamlined reverse logistics

Key Performance Indicators

Measure logistics performance through on-time delivery rates (95%+ target), cost per shipment, inventory turnover (12+ times annually), order accuracy (99.5%+ target), and customer satisfaction scores. Monitor fuel efficiency and asset utilization for comprehensive optimization.
